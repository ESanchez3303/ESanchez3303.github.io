# Another Training Program

### Contact Me
Email: AnotherTrainingProgram@gmail.com

---

### Overview

Another Training Program is a fully local workout and fitness tracking application focused on customizable training sessions and detailed exercise progression.

Features include:

* Creating and organizing workout sessions
* Adding exercises to sessions
* Configuring exercises using:
  * Timed intervals
  * Sets and repetitions
* Playing workout sessions step-by-step
* Tracking exercise performance and progression
* Tracking body weight and BMI
* Tracking nutrition and hydration data:
  * Calories
  * Water intake
  * Additional nutrition metrics
* Exporting and importing complete application data

All data is stored directly on the user's device. No online account or cloud database is required.

---

### Privacy Policy
Effective Date: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how "Another Training Program" handles your information.

### Local Data Storage

Another Training Program stores all application data locally on your device.

This may include:

* Workout sessions
* Exercises and exercise settings
* Exercise progress and performance history
* Body weight and BMI information
* Nutrition and hydration tracking data

No cloud storage or online database is used by default.

### Data Collection

The application does not collect personal information from users and does not transmit app data to external servers.

### Export and Import Features

Users may export and import their application data manually for backup or transfer purposes.

Exported files are fully controlled by the user.

### Data Sharing

Another Training Program does not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties.

### Your Control

Because all information is stored locally on your device, you maintain full control over your data.

You may delete app data at any time by removing exported files or uninstalling the application.

### Security

While reasonable care is taken to protect local application data, users are responsible for securing their own devices and backups.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
